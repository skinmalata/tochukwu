TemplateMonster vs ThemeForest: Which Has Better Support?

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

When buying templates, good support can save you hours of frustration. Let's compare the support offerings of TemplateMonster and ThemeForest to see which comes out on top.

Support Channels

TemplateMonster

Offers multiple support channels:

  • 24/7 Live Chat (fastest response)
  • Support Ticket System
  • Phone Support (in some regions)
  • Extensive Documentation
  • Video Tutorials

ThemeForest

Support depends on the individual author:

  • Author-specific Support Forums
  • Item Comments Section
  • Email Support (author-dependent)
  • Envato Tuts+ Tutorials
  • Community Forums

Response Times

TemplateMonster

Live chat: Immediate. Ticket system: 2-24 hours. Phone: Immediate during business hours.

ThemeForest

Varies by author. Top authors: 24-48 hours. Some authors: 3-5 days or no response.

Documentation Quality

TemplateMonster

Standardized documentation template. Every theme includes installation guide, feature overview, and FAQ section.

ThemeForest

Quality varies by author. Popular themes have excellent docs. Smaller authors may have minimal documentation.

Refund Policy

TemplateMonster

14-day refund policy. If the template doesn't work as described, you get your money back.

ThemeForest

Envato's refund policy covers "not as described" or broken items. Disputes are handled by Envato team.

Winner: TemplateMonster

TemplateMonster wins on support consistency. 24/7 live chat and standardized docs make it easier for beginners. ThemeForest's support quality depends entirely on which author you buy from.
