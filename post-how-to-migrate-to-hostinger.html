How to Migrate Your Site to Hostinger for Free

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

Hostinger offers free website migration for WordPress sites. Whether you're moving from Bluehost, GoDaddy, or any other host, I'll show you how to migrate your site to Hostinger with zero downtime.

Method 1: Hostinger's Free Migration Service

Hostinger offers free professional migration for up to 3 websites (on Premium plans and above). Here's how:

1Sign up for Hostinger

Create your Hostinger account and select the Premium or Business plan to get free migration service.

2Request Migration in hPanel

Log in to your Hostinger hPanel, go to "Migrate Website" and fill out the request form with your current hosting details.

3Wait for Completion

Hostinger's team typically completes migrations within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email when done.

Method 2: Manual Migration with Plugin

If you prefer to do it yourself:

  1. Install "All-in-One WP Migration" plugin on your current site
  2. Export your site (file size under 512MB is free)
  3. Set up WordPress on Hostinger
  4. Install the same plugin on the new site
  5. Import your backup file
