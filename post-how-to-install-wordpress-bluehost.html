How to Install WordPress on Bluehost in Under 5 Minutes

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

Bluehost makes installing WordPress incredibly easy with their one-click installation. In this guide, I'll walk you through setting up your first WordPress site in under 5 minutes.

What You'll Need

  • A Bluehost account (sign up here)
  • A domain name (or use the free one from Bluehost)
  • 5 minutes of your time

Step 1: Log in to Bluehost

1Access your Bluehost dashboard

After signing up, log in to your Bluehost account. You'll see the onboarding wizard if it's your first time.

Step 2: Click "Create Website"

2Start the WordPress installation

Click the "Create Website" button. Bluehost will ask you to select a platform—choose WordPress.

Step 3: Choose Your Domain

3Select or create your domain

Either use the free domain included with your plan, or enter an existing domain you've already purchased.

Step 4: Configure WordPress Settings

4Set up your site details

  1. Enter your site name
  2. Choose a tagline (or leave blank)
  3. Select your WordPress username
  4. Create a strong password

Step 5: Access Your WordPress Admin

5Log in to WordPress

Your WordPress site is now ready! Access your admin at yoursite.com/wp-admin with the credentials you just created.
