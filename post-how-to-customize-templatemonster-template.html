How to Customize a TemplateMonster Template for Your Brand

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

Buying a template is just the first step. To make it truly yours, you need to customize it to match your brand. Here's a step-by-step guide to customizing TemplateMonster templates.

Step 1: Define Your Brand Guidelines

Before touching code, gather:

  • Brand colors (hex codes)
  • Logo (SVG or PNG with transparent background)
  • Font choices (Google Fonts recommendations)
  • Brand voice and messaging

Step 2: Replace Default Content

Start by replacing placeholder content:

  • Update all text with your own copy
  • Replace images with your own (optimize to WebP format)
  • Update contact information
  • Remove unnecessary demo pages

Step 3: Customize Colors and Fonts

Use the theme's built-in customization panel or edit CSS:

  • Change primary and secondary colors
  • Update heading and body fonts
  • Adjust button styles and hover effects
  • Match your brand's visual identity

Step 4: Optimize Images

TemplateMonster templates come with high-res demo images. Optimize yours:

  • Compress images to under 200KB
  • Use proper dimensions (don't scale down in HTML)
  • Add alt text for SEO
  • Use WebP format with JPEG fallback

Step 5: Test Responsiveness

Check your customizations on:

  • Mobile devices (iPhone, Android)
  • Tablets (iPad, Android tablets)
  • Different screen resolutions
  • Various browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)
