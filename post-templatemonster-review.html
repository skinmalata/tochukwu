TemplateMonster Review: Is It Worth Buying Templates Here?

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

TemplateMonster has been around since 2000 and offers 26,000+ templates for every platform. But is it worth buying templates here? Let me share my experience.

Quick answer: Yes, especially if you need templates for multiple platforms (Shopify, WordPress, HTML, etc.) or want a membership option.

The Good Stuff

1. Multi-Platform Support

Unlike ThemeForest (mostly WordPress), TemplateMonster offers templates for Shopify, WooCommerce, PrestaShop, Joomla, Drupal, HTML5, and more.

2. Membership Option

$299/year gets you unlimited downloads. If you're an agency or build multiple sites, this pays for itself quickly.

3. Curated Quality

Every template is tested by their team before approval. More consistent quality than open marketplaces.

4. 24/7 Live Chat Support

Real humans available around the clock. Much better than ticket-based support systems.

The Not-So-Good Stuff

1. Smaller Selection

26,000 templates vs ThemeForest's 50,000+. Less variety in design styles.

2. Less Community Buzz

Fewer user reviews and discussions. Harder to find honest feedback from other buyers.

3. Membership Auto-Renewal

Watch out for auto-renewal on memberships. Cancel in advance if you don't need it.

4. Older Template Designs

Some templates haven't been updated in years. Always check the "Last Update" date.

Membership $299/year Agencies, frequent buyers
