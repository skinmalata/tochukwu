Shopify vs WooCommerce: Which Is Better for Your Online Store?

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

Shopify and WooCommerce are the two most popular e-commerce platforms. I've built stores on both, and each has its strengths. Let me break down the comparison to help you choose.

Quick answer: Choose Shopify if you want an all-in-one solution with zero tech headaches. Choose WooCommerce if you want full control and already have WordPress hosting.

Head-to-Head Comparison

Feature Shopify WooCommerce
Starting Price $39/month Free (pay for hosting)
Hosting Included ✅ Yes ❌ No (need hosting)
Setup Difficulty Easy (1-2 hours) Medium (4-8 hours)
Maintenance Zero (managed) Self-managed
Payment Fees 0.5%-2% (if not using Shopify Payments) None (your choice)
Scalability Excellent Depends on hosting

Who Should Choose Shopify?

  • Non-techies who want simplicity
  • Businesses that want hosting included
  • Stores planning to scale quickly
  • Merchants who want 24/7 support

Who Should Choose WooCommerce?

  • Developers who want full control
  • Existing WordPress sites adding e-commerce
  • Stores on a tight budget (after hosting)
  • Businesses with custom functionality needs
