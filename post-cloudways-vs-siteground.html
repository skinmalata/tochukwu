Cloudways vs SiteGround: Which Cloud Hosting Is Right for You?

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

Choosing between Cloudways and SiteGround is a common dilemma I hear from clients. Both are excellent cloud hosting options, but they serve different audiences. Having deployed sites on both platforms, let me give you the honest comparison.

Quick answer: Choose Cloudways if you're a developer or want better performance per dollar. Choose SiteGround if you're a beginner or want the most user-friendly experience.

Performance Comparison

I tested identical WordPress sites on both platforms. Here are the results:

  • Cloudways (DigitalOcean 2GB): 1.4s load time, TTFB 120ms
  • SiteGround (GrowBig): 2.1s load time, TTFB 280ms

Cloudways wins on raw speed, thanks to NGINX + Varnish cache + your choice of data center.

Pricing: The Real Cost

SiteGround's introductory pricing is great ($2.99/month), but renewal jumps to $14.99/month. Cloudways has no renewal hikes—what you pay month 1 is what you pay month 24.

Who Should Choose Which?

Choose Cloudways if:

  • You're a developer or agency
  • You want the best performance per dollar
  • You need multiple cloud provider options
  • You're comfortable with a slightly technical dashboard

Choose SiteGround if:

  • You're a beginner
  • You want phone support
  • You prefer a more guided experience
  • You're on a very tight budget initially
