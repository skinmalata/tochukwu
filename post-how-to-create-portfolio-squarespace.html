How to Create a Portfolio Site on Squarespace (Step-by-Step)

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

A portfolio site is essential for creative professionals. Squarespace makes it easy to build a stunning portfolio without coding. Follow this step-by-step guide to launch your portfolio in under an hour.

Step 1: Choose the Right Template

Start by browsing Squarespace's portfolio templates. Popular choices include:

  • For photographers: "Marta" or "Darwin"
  • For designers: "Bailard" or "Paco"
  • For agencies: "Bedford" or "Montauk"

Step 2: Set Up Your Site Structure

Every portfolio needs these core pages:

  • Home: Hero section with your best work
  • Portfolio: Gallery of your projects
  • About: Your story and expertise
  • Contact: Easy way for clients to reach you

Step 3: Upload Your Work

Use Squarespace's gallery blocks to showcase your projects. Optimize images to 2000px width for crisp display. Add project descriptions, client names, and your role.

Pro Tip: Use Lightbox

Enable lightbox mode so visitors can view your work full-screen without leaving the page.

Step 4: Customize Your Branding

Add your logo, choose brand colors, and select fonts that match your style. Squarespace's style editor makes this easy with real-time previews.

Step 5: Optimize for SEO

Add meta descriptions, alt text to images, and clean URLs. Squarespace has built-in SEO tools to help you rank.

Step 6: Connect Your Domain

Either buy a domain through Squarespace or connect an existing one. Make sure to set up SSL (included free with Squarespace).
