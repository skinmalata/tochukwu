Shopify vs Wix vs Squarespace: E-commerce Platforms Compared

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

Choosing an e-commerce platform is a big decision. Shopify, Wix, and Squarespace are the three most popular options for small businesses. Having built stores on all three, let me give you the detailed comparison.

Quick verdict: Choose Shopify for pure e-commerce. Choose Squarespace for design-focused stores. Choose Wix for simplicity and flexibility.

Head-to-Head Comparison

Feature Shopify Wix Squarespace
Starting Price $39/month $27/month $33/month
E-commerce Focus 100% E-commerce Part Website Builder Part Website Builder
Products Limit Unlimited Unlimited Unlimited
Design Flexibility Medium High (Drag & Drop) Medium (Templates)
Payment Processing Shopify Payments Wix Payments Squarespace Payments

Who Should Choose Which?

Choose Shopify if:

  • You're serious about e-commerce
  • You plan to scale to high volumes
  • You want the best app ecosystem
  • You need multi-channel selling (Amazon, eBay, etc.)

Choose Wix if:

  • You want total design freedom
  • You're on a tighter budget
  • You need both a website and store
  • You're a beginner with no tech skills

Choose Squarespace if:

  • Design is your top priority
  • You sell creative/artistic products
  • You want beautiful templates out of the box
