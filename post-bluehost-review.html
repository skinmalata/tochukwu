Bluehost Review: Is It Still the Best Hosting for Beginners?

By Tochukwu Mesigo | Web Developer & SEO Specialist

Bluehost has been around since 2003 and powers over 2 million websites worldwide. They're officially recommended by WordPress.org, but is their hosting still good in 2026? As someone who's worked with hundreds of client sites, let me give you the honest truth.

Short answer: Bluehost is still a solid choice for beginners on a tight budget. But if your site generates revenue, there are better options.

The Good Stuff

1. Extremely Beginner-Friendly

Bluehost's onboarding is excellent. They walk you through:

  • One-click WordPress installation
  • Free domain for the first year
  • Simple control panel (customized cPanel)
  • 24/7 phone and chat support

2. Low Introductory Pricing

At $2.95/month (intro price), Bluehost is one of the cheapest ways to get a WordPress site online. This includes a free domain and SSL certificate.

3. Official WordPress Recommendation

Bluehost has been on WordPress.org's official recommendation list for years. They've optimized their servers specifically for WordPress.

The Not-So-Good Stuff

1. Slow Performance

In my speed tests, Bluehost sites typically load in 2.5-4 seconds—significantly slower than SiteGround or Cloudways. This can hurt your SEO and conversion rates.

2. Aggressive Upselling

Bluehost pushes a lot of add-ons during checkout: SiteLock security, SEO tools, CodeGuard backups. Most of these you don't need.

3. Renewal Pricing

Like most hosts, the introductory price jumps at renewal. $2.95/month becomes $10.99/month after the first term.

4. No Free Backups on Basic Plan

Unlike SiteGround which includes daily backups on all plans, Bluehost only includes backups on higher-tier plans.

Who Should Use Bluehost?

✅ Complete beginners - Easy onboarding and setup
✅ Hobby bloggers - Low cost for personal sites
✅ Students - Learning WordPress on a budget

❌ Business sites - Slow speed hurts conversions
❌ E-commerce - Need faster, more secure hosting
❌ High-traffic sites - Will outgrow Bluehost quickly
